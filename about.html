What We Do

Executive Housing, Managed.

Vivo Partners is a specialized executive housing operator connecting property owners with the top tier of the traveling workforce. We manage furnished residential properties for high-caliber professionals — from specialized surgeons to corporate executives — who require a quiet, private residence.

By prioritizing minimal-occupancy placements, we ensure a stable, high-end environment that maintains the highest standards of property care and minimizes impact on the home.

Our Vision

Long-Lasting Partnerships.

To create long-lasting housing partnerships that bring financial stability to communities while providing safe, comfortable homes for our guests.

Our Mission

Deliver Excellence.

To deliver excellence in professional asset management by prioritizing the things that matter most to property owners:

  • On-time, guaranteed rental payments — every month, regardless of our occupancy
  • Professional guest vetting for your safety and peace of mind
  • Long-term partnerships that minimize turnover and maximize occupancy
  • Transparent communication and full documentation available on request
